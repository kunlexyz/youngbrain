INSTRUCTIONAL GUIDELINES FOR USING THE APP.

The App is Primarily designed to bring about a pragmatic approach in solving the problem of Aparthy in the teaching learning process of biology thus enhancing students excellent performances in relevant examinations.
The App contains important biology topics in a three volume course
  • Each topic contains
  • Background information
  • General questions
  • Practical Activities
  • SSCE past questions
A user of the app should refresh on the topic by reading the background information,
Answer the questions and watch it marked at once by the app
means you got the answer right while means you got it wrong.
A user is expected to do more study, research and consultations on his or her own to search out the correct answer for any question marked wrong, feed in the answer again.
Such process enhances learning and retentivity.
Some questions are tagged TAR(Teachers Attention Required) These are questions that require teachers explanation or some level of expression for the answer. Users are to answer such questions on their own and consult their teachers or youngbrain consult for answer verification via Whatsapp or any other telecommuting device.
Users can also interact with youngbrain or with teachers who are regarded as partners with youngbrain in the same way.
You can use the app online and offline.

Opening the App.

Note: The App can be downloaded via Whatsapp or Facebook link but it cannot open until it is activated.
The App opens When it is activated through an activation code provided by youngbrain or by direct online payment as stated in the app.
When the app is opened, click on menu to see various options. Then click on either of the books or on the options you want to see or work with.
