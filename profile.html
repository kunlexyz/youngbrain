About Us

- A Limited liability Company incorporated in 2003 to take printing and publishing to a digital age level.
- We communicate ideas and expressions of creative minds in prints to reach a large target audience
- We are in business to give excellent expressions and finesse to disclosed innovations, reasoning and intellectual presentations.
- In young brain, we render quality printing and publishing services with precision, in conformity to 21st century printing technology.  

OUR CORE VALUES

Honesty and sincerity
Prompt delivery
Fidelity
Excellence
Humility 

OUR VISION STATEMENT

- To ensure that every print from us has a touch of class and excellence.
- To promote a reading culture through learning stimulating publications.
- To boost healthy development of young minds through the wonder of books.
- To stand out from the band wagon and carve a niche of distinction in our business ideals. 

MISSION STATEMENT

- To see good books not only published but made accessible to young minds.
- To ensure that all our productions and publications conform to latest innovations in printing. 

AS A PUBLISHING FIRM

WE ARTICULATE and convert what has been penned down in ink into a printed material (book) with lasting quality.
WE HAVE VESTED interest in the publication of learning material and any other useful publication, especially those that will enhance healthy development of young minds.
WE COMBINE simplicity and sequential order in presentation of information, instruction and ideas.
WE CHOOSE the right color application and combination in our book publication in order to make reading interesting and arouse learning skills.

OUR WORK does not stop at publication of books and materials, we follow them up through our marketing networks till they reach their target audience
IN ORDER to keep pace with the present information age, some of our publications are recorded in CD’s so that the reader can also listen to them.
