Preface

“Biology- the science of life , should be the most interesting subject to student at least for the knowledge it supplies about life”.
Many students would not like to accept the statement above as statement of fact because of their lack of interest in the subject. This informs the need and the quest for books that will provide a practical application of biological concepts to real day to day activities of students. More often than not, students forget important biological concepts and illustrations, just because they wait for a notice of a test or examination. Such problem and other related academic vices which has not promoted students good performances in internal and external examinations can be eliminated where there is a provision of a book that gives a thoroughy attention to practical activities and “work to do” in biology. Such material makes students understand biological concepts with ease as they see it's relevance and practical applications in their day to day lives. The book New Senior Biology: practical Activity and Work book which in three series or volume, is one such material.

EACH VOLUME OF THIS BOOK IS DIVIDED INTO THREE PARTS.

  1. The background information in summary, which gives a brief review of the topic under consideration.
  2. Practical activity section: a section that exposes related practical questions the student can encounter in SSCE and G.C.E. Examinations.
  3. General questions and SSCE past questions.
A proper use of the three-volume of this book will equip students for their SSCE and other related examinations even from the scratch. As students will find the book useful and expository, as a complete examination and continuous assessment aid, teachers alike, will find the book a comprehensive “ready made” and reliable evaluation “tool”
