Privacy Policy for New Senior School Biology Practical and Activity Workbook

Effective Date: October 7, 2023

Introduction

Welcome to New Senior School Biology Practical and Activity Workbook, a mobile application designed to provide educational content and services for students and children. We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our young users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard personal information from children and students using our app.

1. Information We Collect

- 1.1. Information from Users

- Children's Information: We may collect limited personal information from children and students, such as a username, age, and learning preferences, but only to the extent necessary for the operation of the app and in compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

- 1.2. Automatically Collected Information

- Usage Information: We may collect data related to the child's or student's usage of the app, including progress in educational activities, achievements, and interaction with content.

2. How We Use Children's Information

We use children's information for the following purposes:

- To Provide Educational Services: To offer personalized educational content and activities to children and students.

- To Improve and Analyze: To analyze app usage, enhance the learning experience, and troubleshoot issues.

- To Communicate: To communicate with parents or legal guardians about their child's progress or activity within the app.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or disclose children's personal information to third parties except as described below:

- With Parental Consent: We may share children's information with third-party service providers, such as cloud hosting services, but only after obtaining verifiable parental consent.

- Legal Compliance: We may disclose children's information when required by law or to protect legal rights.

4. Parental Rights and Choices

- Access and Deletion: Parents or legal guardians have the right to access their child's personal information stored in our app and request its deletion. They can do so by contacting us.

- Consent Revocation: Parents or legal guardians can revoke their consent for the collection and use of their child's personal information at any time by contacting us.

5. Security

We take security measures to protect children's personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is entirely secure but we do our best to ensure maximum security.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or the law. We will notify parents or legal guardians of any material changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your child's information, please contact us.

By using our app, parents or legal guardians acknowledge that they have read and agree to this Privacy Policy on behalf of their child.
